Bali

Indonesia

Bali is a Western Indonesian island with mountains, forests and lakes which complement the beautiful beaches. There are three languages spoken in Bali and the culture is a mix of influences from Chinese, Indian and Hindu cultures.

One of the things that you can do in Bali is go and visit the many beaches that are there. You can participate in watersports activities like surfing or book a scuba diving session or just chill by yourself in the water and relax on the beach with a book. Another thing that you can do is go through the forests and more specifically the sacred monkey forest sanctuary where not only can you see the monkeys but bookfor ziplining and other fun activities.
